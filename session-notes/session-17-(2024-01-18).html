Session 17 (2024-01-18)

Session Summary

The party engages in a series of rapid transitions and combats across various planes. Starting with a dragon attack as they attempt to use the "Dragon's Gate," they find themselves battling in Mars' canyon (Akatun) against a ratman on a scorpion. Abruptly, the environment shifts to Fleshtown (Octurne), challenging the group with its noxious atmosphere and an encounter with a Nightgaunt. The scene transitions yet again to a desolate moon (Chi-ox), where we face a Cairn Wight. The chaotic journey through portals underscores their quest's unpredictability and the vastness of the multiverse.

Recap of Last Session

After defeating a Hieracosphinx on the roof of The Offering Spire, we successfully summon "She Who Walks Through Seasons" using a great spore horn made of living fungus. Following the summoning, we engage with Kappas near a sacred spring, mistakenly fighting guardians before realizing a peaceful approach was needed. Collecting sacred water, we return to Loskialua where A-Lay-Lay is cured of the Obnubilate Curse with a newly prepared potion. The Shadewither Key reveals a vision of a portal hub to Nishara, hinting at vast knowledge and a connection to other realms. A-Lay-Lay proposes a method to return us home via a portal on Galerion, linked with the Shadewither Key, despite temporal discrepancies suggesting a journey back in time. The session concludes with plans to explore the Old Gate and #LEVEL FIVE BOIIIIIIIII
