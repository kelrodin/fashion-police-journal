Session 11 (2023-11-30)

Session Summary

We explore a jungle, spotting pyramid structures and encountering exotic creatures, including a tree fisher and a snail-ipede. Crossing a rust-red river, we fight off amphibious threats. Reaching a hilltop, we engage in combat with catlike creatures near an ancient elven statue. Taking a gondola, we're attacked by pterodactyls but manage to reach a pyramid city where a crowd of Elves awaits us!

Recap of Last Session

We encounter traps and a knife ghost in Kaneepo's lab, leading to a battle with a ghost and the Look-see Man. After dealing with them, we explore Kaneepo's cell, uncovering mystical sigils and confronting Kaneepo himself along with his shadow dog. Despite Kaneepo's efforts, we kick his ass, learn hints about our powers, and escape the dungeon, following the Shadewither Key to a shadow version of the Seven Arches. Entering a portal, we're transported through an Aiudara to a...jungle? The gate platform has evidence of dragons. We level up!
