Session 24 (2024-03-07)

Session Summary

We nearly kill Capstan Swabby, but are stopped by the ghost of Captain Adney Tinhill! Explaining his unfinished journey due to Flobius, a Nyad serving the water lord Arangadon, Tinhill proposes a deal to aid in his journey's completion in exchange for dealing with Flobius, who binds him with her magic.
Equipped with darkvision goggles, a serpent dagger, and a map to Flobius's shrine, we set sail, encountering ghostly figures and navigating through a chilling temple marked by a blue oroborus of Ragadahn, the Water Lord. Combat ensues against guardian Dragur and Kvernknurr.

Recap of Last Session

We gather up the Erutaki and head to the Observatory. We're ambushed by that damn Dagron, but with the help of the Erutaki, it eats shit. A vision at the Aiudara reveals a connection to the Worldwound Scar in Sarkoris, prompting a journey across the Lake of Mists and Veils to the Junlan Wrecks. There, we discover a fine ship The Mermaid's Tears and are ambushed by a Capstan Swabby; combat on this icy vessel!
