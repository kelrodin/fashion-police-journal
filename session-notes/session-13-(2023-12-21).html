Session 13 (2023-12-21)

Session Summary

We confront animated statues in a room of bas-relief figures, battling their psychic attacks with a mix of magic, thievery, and brute force. After defeating them and exploring further, we encounter skeletons armed with ancient weapons, engaging in another combat that we navigate successfully. Discovering a unique crossbow and a special buckler among the ruins, we interact with mushroom leshi, warning us of a snake. Encountering quill snakes, identified as Scale Scribes, we engage in combat, overcoming their poison and magical attacks.

Recap of Last Session

We arrive in Castrovel's pyramid city, Loskialua, greeted by elves and led by A-Lay-Lay to an inn. Benly the errand boy shares local stories, including one about the goddamn psychically enhanced apes!
At night, we're taken to a dome with a huge telescope, meeting Gentheel and the council. Discussing the Missing Moment and the Shadewither Key's curse, we learn it's controlled by a psychic trigger related to Gentheel's mother. Tasked to find her in The Garden, a place for the city's greatest minds, we descend into a cavern and fight Immoles. After defeating them, we discover a room filled with statues in robes.
