Session 20 (2024-02-08)

Session Summary

In the perpetual darkness of Skywatch, the party navigates to various investigation points, including the Guard Post and Mystic Market, after receiving healing potions from Sakuachi. At the Guard Post, they confront and defeat hobgoblins, discovering one with a gatewalker tattoo named Ghodrak, the leader of the Obsidian Oath. Rescuing Ulikuq from Aaminiut, they return him to the Smiling Wolves Camp, earning a schooner and learning about the Obsidian Oath's entrance through the Gate.

En route to the Mystic Market, we encounter Ishalt, a cursed wolf offering Dao a chance to take on his curse. At the Mystic Market, they find signs of struggle and distress among the inhabitants, setting the stage for further exploration and confrontation.

Recap of Last Session

Sakuachi joins the group, providing healing potions and insights into her mission to investigate Skywatch due to elders' visions. She reveals her gatewalker tattoo, Aqakaru, and shares the goal of finding the Falling Star amid a great beast's threat. The party navigates a computer labeled "Castrovel," linking to their past locations and warning against looking at certain planets. The city, fluctuating between destruction and normalcy, presents a shifting reality, challenged further by an encounter with a magical cat. Rescued by the Smiling Wolves, led by Innumma and Matz Balandim, they're guided through the twisted city using an Astrolabe and offered missions to aid in securing Skywatch.
