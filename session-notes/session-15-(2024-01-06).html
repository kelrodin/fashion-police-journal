Session 15 (2024-01-06)

Session Summary

Returning to Loskialua, we receive healing potions from mushroom leshi and set out to summon "She Who Walks Through Seasons" by making an offering at the abandoned spire, now home to aggressive flora and fauna. Along the way, we encounter a Korrock, defeating it with strategy and force. Ascending the spire, we battle and overcome aggressive vines tentacle monster, utilizing teamwork and heroics to progress up the tower.

Recap of Last Session

In a confrontation with quill snakes (Sneks), our group manages to defeat them with strategic attacks, learning the mechanics of aiding each other in battle. After the fight, we exchange significant memories with telepathic entities for passage, leading us to meet Skyseer Oatia amidst magical, cabbage-like growths. Oatia reveals the Osoyo Protocol related to a blackfrost whale (Osoyo!) from the High Houses Era, responsible for a psychic viral extinction. We learn the Shadewither Key contains the Osoyo Virus, an anti-life curse targeting elves, with hints towards a cure involving "agua deific" and advice to consult "She Who Walks Through Seasons." Selfless Son/Sun deity gave themselves up to ensure Osoyo's imprisonment, but the prison is weakening!
